Networking

Wireless

Wireless (Wi-Fi) is available on main campus by connecting to WolfieNet-Secure with your NetID and NetID password.

For more information on wireless access and locations, please visit:  http://www.stonybrook.edu/it > Services > Wi-Fi (WolfieNet)

Connecting to ResNet

ResNet brings direct ethernet connectivity to the residence halls and public jacks located on main campus. Student need to be sure that their computers are up-to-date with:

  • The latest Microsoft Windows Updates
  • An approved and up-to-date anti-virus program

For more information, please visit:  http://www.stonybrook.edu/it > Services > Network (Wired)
