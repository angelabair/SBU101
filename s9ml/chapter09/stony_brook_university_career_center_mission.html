Stony Brook Career Center Mission

The Career Center at Stony Brook University exists to:

  • EDUCATE students about the career development process and industry options, empowering them to make informed career decisions.
  • PREPARE students for experiential learning (i.e. internships and community service), employment and further education.
  • CONNECT hiring organizations with our diverse student talent.

Our VISION for SBU students

Students will view academic & career planning as intertwined, and take responsibility for their success by:

  • Acquiring knowledge of self and of career options
  • Gaining related experience and establishing professional networks
  • Reflecting on experiences to shape their next steps
  • Committing to ongoing career development and professional growth
